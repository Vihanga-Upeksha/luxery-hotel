Our Story

A legacy of luxury and hospitality.

Since 1998

A Legacy of Luxury

What started as a boutique guesthouse has evolved into the city's premier destination for luxury travelers. Founded by the Ellington family, The Villa was built on the principles of immaculate service and timeless design.

Over the decades, we have hosted royalty, celebrities, and discerning travelers from around the globe, maintaining our reputation as a sanctuary of sophistication.

Responsibility

Committed to Green Luxury

We believe that luxury shouldn't cost the earth. That's why we've implemented a comprehensive sustainability program that includes zero-waste dining, solar energy utilization, and locally sourced amenities.

Our 'Green Villa' initiative ensures that your stay supports local communities and preserves the natural beauty of our surroundings.
